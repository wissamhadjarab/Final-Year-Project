What You Should Know About Zakat

Nisab Threshold

Zakat becomes due once your wealth exceeds the Nisab (value of 612.36g of silver).

Purification

Zakat purifies your wealth and protects it from harm and spiritual loss.

Who Must Pay

Muslims who possess the Nisab for one full Hijri year are obligated to pay Zakat.
